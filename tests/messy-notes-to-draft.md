# Pressure Test: Messy Notes to Draft

## User request

Turn this into a memo:
- customers mad about delay
- beta broke import flow
- fixed now mostly
- don’t overpromise
- team tired
- launch next Tues? maybe
- need to sound calm not defensive

## Expected behavior

- Infer `notes_to_draft`.
- Preserve constraints: calm, not defensive, no overpromising.
- Do not invent exact dates or claims beyond notes.
- Use placeholders or cautious phrasing for uncertain launch date.
- Produce a memo that is clearer than notes but still grounded.

## Failure mode

- Corporate smoothing: “We are excited to announce a seamless improved experience...”
- Inventing metrics, dates, or customer quotes.
